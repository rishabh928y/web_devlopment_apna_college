Maggie Recipe

  1. Fill a pot with 2-3 cups of water and bring to a boil.
  2. Once boiling, add the Maggie masala magic or Maggie noodles whichever you are using
  3. Cook for 3 minutes or until noodles are cooked.
  4. Strain the noodles and rinse with cold water to stop the cooking process.
  5. In another pot, heat 1-2 tablespoons of oil or butter.
  6. Add some veggies like:-
    • Peas
    • Carrot
    • Onion
    • Potatoes
  7. Add the chopped vegetables and saut√© for 2-3 minutes.
  8. Add the cooked noodles and stir to combine.
  9. Add Maggie masala magic or Maggie noodles seasoning and mix well.
  10. Simmer for 1-2 minutes.
  11. Serve hot with your favorite toppings. Enjoy!
